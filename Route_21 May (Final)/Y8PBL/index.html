“In many cities today, traffic congestion and energy waste are serious problems. Too many vehicles cause delays, pollution, and wasted fuel. Energy is also used inefficiently in homes and buildings. Our project explores smart solutions to create a more sustainable future.”

Why It Matters

Reducing traffic congestion and energy waste is important because it helps protect our environment and improve our quality of life. Too many vehicles increase air pollution and waste fuel, while inefficient energy use wastes electricity and natural resources. By using smart technology, we can create cleaner air, save energy, reduce costs, and build a more sustainable and comfortable city for everyone.
