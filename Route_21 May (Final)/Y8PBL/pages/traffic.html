Traffic Congestion

Traffic congestion happens when too many vehicles use the road at the same time. This causes delays, fuel waste, pollution, and stress for people every day.

Problems Caused by Traffic Congestion

Air Pollution

Vehicle smoke increases harmful gases in the air and affects health.

Fuel Waste

Cars waste fuel while waiting in traffic for a long time.

Time Loss

People spend extra time travelling because roads are overcrowded.

Noise Pollution

Constant honking and traffic sounds create noise and stress people.

Road Accidents

Heavy traffic increases the risk of crashes and unsafe driving.
