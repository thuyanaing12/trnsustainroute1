Energy Resilience

Energy waste happens when electricity or fuel is used unnecessarily or inefficiently. This leads to higher costs, pollution, and damage to the environment.

Problems Caused by Energy Resilience

Environmental Damage

Excessive energy use contributes to global warming and environmental problems.

Resource Depletion

Wasting energy uses up natural resources like coal, oil, and gas more quickly.

Fuel Waste

Leaving lights, appliances, or vehicles running wastes fuel and energy resources.

Higher Electricity Costs

Using more energy than needed increases electricity and fuel expenses.

Air Pollution

Wasting energy increases harmful gases released into the air and affects people's health.
